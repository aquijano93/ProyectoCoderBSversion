Founder


Our founder, Andres Quijano better known as BluInk93, started to flirt with needles and ink two years ago, specialized on dotshading and blackwork, he loves to create dark designs, old school guy, he only use black ink to create his whole pieces. He loves anime so if you want to get your favourite character you can have it for sure. The reason why BluInk93 Supply is now open, his commit with this community made him to get involve in supply selling, one of the few stores in this area, trying to open road for the new ones. Creating this space just for tattoo lovers and colleagues. Nowadays he's trying to make some deals with national producers, to create his own supplies brand. If you are a tattoer you can follow him on YouTube to gets some tips. And they say that a tattoer school coming soon so get ready for it. He is shaking the tattoo market in Uruguay so more people can have more opportunities in this tattoo's world.
